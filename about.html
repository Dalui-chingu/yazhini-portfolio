Who am I

I am Yazhini, born in Tamil nadu, India. I like artistry because it lets me express myself in ways words can't. It feels like a space where I can truly be myself, creating something unique that reflects my thoughts and emotions. I enjoy the freedom to explore ideas, experiment with colors, and bring imagination to life. Artistry challenges me to think differently, and I love how it connects me with beauty and meaning in the world around me. It's not just a hobby—it's how I explore, grow, and find joy in creating.
